Introduce

Welcome to Binh An Village Hotel, a tranquil and serene retreat nestled near the picturesque Tuyen Lam Lake in Vietnam. Our hotel is the perfect destination for travelers seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Surrounded by lush greenery and stunning mountain views, our hotel offers a unique blend of modern amenities and traditional Vietnamese hospitality.

Our spacious and well-appointed rooms are designed to provide maximum comfort and relaxation for our guests. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and free Wi-Fi. Guests can also enjoy stunning views of the lake and surrounding mountains from their

private balconies. At Binh An Village Hotel, we pride ourselves on offering a range of facilities to make your stay as comfortable as possible. Our hotel features a swimming pool, spa, restaurant, and bar, all designed to cater to your every need. We also offer a range of outdoor activities such as hiking, cycling, and fishing, allowing guests to explore the natural beauty of the area.

Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway or a family vacation, Binh An Village Hotel is the perfect choice. Book your stay with us today and experience the beauty and tranquility of Tuyen Lam Lake.
